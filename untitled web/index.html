A Delightful Experience in Laos

Where colonial charm meets modern comfort

Sengahloune Resort & Villa is your ideal pick to explore beautiful Southern Laos. Located in the heart of the 4000 islands, close to the Mekong river, Liphee waterfall, Khone Pa Soy waterfall and the Southernmost point of Laos.

Blending colonial charm with modern comfort, we offer high-quality accommodation, dining, and hospitality services.

Committed to sustainable and excellent service, we're your perfect gateway to discover the natural beauty and cultural richness of Southern Laos.
